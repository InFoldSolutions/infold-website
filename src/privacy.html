Privacy Policy

Personal data

InFold had never collected and will never collect any personal data.

In future InFold may collect browser version, platform name and display settings. This information is needed for decision on implementing new features, removing unused features or suggesting default settings for new users. It will happen only with your permission.

Third party services

InFold uses Chrome (Chromium) or WebExtensions Storage Sync API for storing user's settings.

Donations are processed by Patreon and payment processors chosen by that organization.
